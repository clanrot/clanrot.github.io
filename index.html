Rebellion of Thunder - Portuguese QuakeWorld Clan

About

Rebellion of Thunder (RoT) is a Portuguese QuakeWorld Clan.
It was founded by ztranger in early 1998.

The clan was initially created with Portuguese players from Madeira Island, but is now a borderless gathering of players from multiple nationalities.
